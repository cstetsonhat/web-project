About

This website was created as a student assignment for Basics of Web Design taught by Dr. Mary L. Smith at the University of Virginia. It is intended to be a resource for students interested in post-graduate studies in IT and Cybersecurity in Virginia and online, as those are most applicable to most of my classmates. I have shared resources on education and financial aid or training programs that I have found through web research.

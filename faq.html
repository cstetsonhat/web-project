Faqs:

  1. Q: Is the information given on this website comprehensive? In other words, is this all of the information that is available on all resources regarding post-graduate programs and resources in Virginia and online?

    A: Absolutely not! There is so much information available on all of the wonderful opportunities, that this is a starting point. From here, you can continue down “the rabbit hole” to find other terrific programs and sources of financial help.

  2. Q: Is it possible to get a Master’s degree in IT or Cybersecurity and not have to worry about continuing education afterwards?

    A: The reality of a career in IT or Cybersecurity means embracing the fact that technology continues to evolve and change – quickly, in fact. Choosing a career in a fast-changing field means committing to a path of life-long learning. Earning an advanced degree is a terrific way to bolster your position in the field, but making this choice also means that you will need to continue to invest in training and education throughout your time in this career.
